# Gameplay
applause.|On the ranking screen after clearing a map.
comboburst.|For multiple sounds, use comboburst-{n}.wav.\nIn the skin.ini, if CustomComboBurstSounds is set to 1, comboburst-{n}.wav must be used.|True
combobreak.|Plays when player breaks a combo of 20 or more.
failsound.|Failing a map at any point.
sectionpass.|Halfway through a break with high HP remaining.
sectionfail.|Halfway through a break with low HP remaining.
pause-loop.|Plays when the game is paused.\nThis sound is looped.\nFades out when the client loses focus.
